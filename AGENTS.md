## Claude Code Rules

- Follow `AGENTS.md` core rules.
- Do not output or store secret values.
- Do not create or modify Cloudflare Pages, DNS, or deployment configuration without explicit human approval.
- Keep tasks small and PR-based.
- When blocked, isolate only the blocked item and continue other work.
- For public content, ensure no internal URLs, IPs, family information, or management links are exposed.
